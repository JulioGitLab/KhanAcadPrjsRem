Travel to... "La Chingada"

La Chingada is the ranch of Mexico's president, Andrés Manuel López Obrador. Located at 284 km north of Tuxtla Gutiérrez, the Chiapas State Capital. The ranch is close to the entrance of the municipal head of Palenque, a Chiapas Municipality.

The house has a modest facade, without any luxury, with a black gate. It is an approximate area of three hectares, where you can see a lagoon and a rustic road guided by coconut palm trees that leads to a house made of concrete with a green roof, in the middle of a wooded area.

But this is just one of the Palenque's tourist attractions! Of mayan origin, it is a city located close to the Usumacinta river, and it's main attraction is the Palenque's archaeological area. A few kilometers away you can also find the Agua Clara ecotouristic center, some popular waterfalls (Misol-Ha and Agua Azul) and the Chacamax (Nututun) river.

Palenque's places to visit:

  • La Chingada
  • Palenque's archaeological area
  • Usumacinta river
  • Agua Clara
  • Misol-Ha
  • Agua Azul
  • Chacamax (Nututun) river
